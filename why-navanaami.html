CEO's Statement

Sustainable practices

We, at Navanaami, believe in building homes which are the embodiment of great design and luxury.

While it is important for us to craft unparalleled experiences through our works, we do it with the environmental best practices in mind. Our homes are not only built in but also built around spaces surrounded by the beautiful gifts of nature and we aspire to keep it that way. We only build with the best materials and using the best practices that can help us in this initiative. We implement rainwater harvesting, afforestation programs, eco-friendly waste disposal and sanitation programs in all our projects.

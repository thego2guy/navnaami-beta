Project Info
2BHK, 2.5BHK, 3BHK

Spacious opulent apartments constructed to best encapsulate all that one’s heart could desire and imagination could capture. These apartment houses (available in 2, 2 & half and 3 bedroom options) will mesmerize you. The sheen of its granite countertops, commodious lounging & dining areas, generous balconies, pristine spa-like restrooms will leave you awe-struck. Covered with sublime green foliage in all directions the residence opens up to a gorgeous veranda. The bright light of the sun will glaze over your skin, fresh air will fill your lungs - that's how everyday life begins for people who live at Navanaami Platina.
